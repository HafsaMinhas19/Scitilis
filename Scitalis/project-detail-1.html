The name Scitalis comes from a medieval bestiary and references a serpent so beautiful that it paralyzes anyone who sees it.

At Scitalis we aim to not just create beautiful designs but to instill meaning into those designs. every choice we make has meaning. The colors we use, the shapes we use, the typefaces we choose, and even the layouts or formats we use have meaning.

We want the work we create to be things that you are proud of and can proudly use or display.

Our Process starts with understanding you and what you need. Sometimes you have a challenge/opportunity/dream that you just need a little help solving/taking advantage of/or achieving. Once we understand that we do research and ask a questions. Sometimes those questions are why? or What do you hope for? or how do you see this affecting you or your business? Then we will ideate. Test it with you. then iterate and test again till we get it right.
